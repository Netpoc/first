    About Πέτρος Inc.

    Netpoc Inc. is an innovation company built on the premise of asnwering critical business questions through continuos research and devlopement. It is our core believe that the African private sector has a bigger role to play in the development of the Africa that we all desire. We believe this can be achieved by truly developing solutions that are tailored to the unique challenges common to our beloved continent.

    We are obejective in our approach to challenges and our designs follows first principles in identifying critical path to achieving success. This is why we are positioned to service busniesses in diverse industries and sectors. We believe there will always be challenges that requires critcal, innovative, value driven solutions. We therefore strive to take a walk in our client's shoes

    Our Industries

    Agriculture

    Netpoc AgroTech is on a mission to join the global effort of eradicating poverty in Sub-Saharan Africa through real application of various inclusive business models to creates a win-win market situation that benefits both small holder farmers and our business alike. With a keen focus on the future and alignment with the government mission to diversifying the economy, Netpoc Agrotech wants to be strategically positioned to meet the local and international demand for various farm products.

    Software and DevOps

    Information Technology happens to be our core competence and the platform on which most of our services and offering would leverage on. Sustainable business models and solutions require actionable insight to guide strategic decision. These actionable insights are powered by intelligently written softwares.
